**Concept Medical Group** is a 2008-founded medical-device company headquartered in Surat, India, with subsidiaries in Tampa (US) and the Netherlands. They are best known for:

## 2. Content fundamentals (voice &amp; tone)

The brand's voice in their own materials is **declarative, mission-driven, and clinically confident** — short sentences, recurring rhetorical anchors, and named values.

**Principles**
- **First-person plural ("we", "our")** when speaking from the company; **third-person clinical** in evidence/trial contexts.
- **No emoji.** Ever. This is a medical-device brand audited by FDA / CE / PMDA — emoji compromise credibility.
- **Title Case** for headlines and section titles (e.g. "From the MD's Desk", "Our Presence"). **SENTENCE CASE** for body. **ALL CAPS** reserved for brand-book labels and eyebrow tags ("LOGO USAGE DO'S", "CORPORATE TYPOGRAPHY").
- **Named pillars.** Their materials use a four-line credo: **Our Mission / Our Promise / Our Essence / Our Vibe** — preserve this rhythm when writing about the company.
- **Short, declarative pull-quotes** carry emotional weight: _"Every Delay Is A Cost"_, _"Going the extra mile"_, _"Make in India for the World"_. Use sparingly, attributed.
- Numbers are load-bearing — **75+ countries, 100+ patents, since 2008** — call them out as stat blocks rather than burying them in prose.
- Avoid breathless adjectives ("revolutionary", "groundbreaking") more than once per page; let evidence (trial names, patient numbers, regulatory milestones) do the persuading.

**Vocabulary cues to keep**
- "Drug delivery technology", "endovascular therapy", "vascular interventions"
- "Patient outcomes", "clinical evidence", "long-term"
- "Concepts into reality", "Going the extra mile", "Every delay is a cost"

**Examples (verbatim from supplied profile)**

## 3. Visual foundations

The CM visual identity is **a single dominant blue, a refined transitional serif, generous white space, and clinical restraint.** No gradients, no illustrative flourishes, no photography of people unless clinically grounded.

### Color
- **One signature brand blue** (`#1D4E9F`, Pantone P 102-8 C) carries 80% of the system. A **navy scale** (`#122248 → #4D7BBE`) provides depth and hover/press states. A **bright cyan-blue** (`#00B7F1`) is the only sanctioned accent.
- **Product sub-brands** each get a single accent color (yellow for MagicTouch, green for Abluminus DES+, etc.) — used as a 1-pixel rule, a small dot, or a product-name color, never as a flooded background.
- **Neutrals** are cool-leaning greys; pure black is avoided in favor of `#0E1119`.
- See `colors_and_type.css` for the full token set.

### Typography
- **Primary brand font: Poppins.** This is what the live website (conceptmedical.com) uses across headings, body, navigation, CTAs, and footer. Loaded as `--font-brand` in `colors_and_type.css`. Weights 400 / 500 / 600 / 700 cover the entire site.
- **Editorial italic accent: Cormorant Garamond.** Reserved for occasional pull-quotes and italic highlights inside Poppins headlines (mirrors the serif italics in the "Concept Medical" wordmark). Apply via `&lt;em class="editorial"&gt;` inside a heading.
- **Japanese subset: Noto Sans JP** (300, brand-supplied `.woff2`). Loaded via `@font-face` and stacked into `--font-brand` so JP characters render correctly inside Poppins-led copy.
- **Wordmark serif (logo only):** Cambria/Garamond family — appears in the supplied logo PNG/PDF files. Do **not** recreate the wordmark in HTML; always use the assets in `assets/`.
- Sentence case for body, Title Case for headlines, ALL CAPS for eyebrows + buttons (.12–.22em tracking).

### Layout
- **Generous white space.** The brand book itself is mostly white. Hero sections breathe; padding scales aggressively at desktop.
- **Two backgrounds only**: white (`--bg`) and deep navy (`--bg-inverse`, `#213E7B`). Section rhythm alternates between them.
- **Centered lockups** are common — corporate, formal, symmetric.
- Containers cap at `1200–1280px`; no full-bleed photography unless the photo is the focal point.

### Backgrounds &amp; imagery
- **Solid blue fields** (`#213E7B`) carry the white-logo lockup. This is the brand's signature treatment.
- **No gradients.** A subtle radial vignette toward the navy is allowed at 4–6% on hero backgrounds; never multi-color gradients.
- **No illustrations.** No hand-drawn iconography, no abstract geometric scribbles.
- Photography should read **clinical, cool-toned, real** — cath-lab, device macros, manufacturing-floor — never warm/lifestyle.

### Borders, radii, shadows
- **Radii are restrained**: 2 / 4 / 8 / 14 / 22 / pill / circle. The **circle** is meaningful (logo mark) — reserve full circles for brand moments.
- **1px borders** at `--neutral-200` carry most card definition; cards rarely use shadow at rest.
- **Shadows are cool-blue tinted** (`rgba(18, 34, 72, 0.10)`) — never warm grey.
- **Inner highlights** (`inset 0 1px 0 rgba(255,255,255,0.6)`) on raised buttons add subtle dimensionality.

### Animation
- **No bounces.** No spring physics.
- Standard transition: `160ms ease` for hover, `240ms cubic-bezier(0.2, 0.8, 0.2, 1)` for layout shifts.
- Hover states: **darken the fill by one step** (`--cm-blue-500 → --cm-blue-600`), not opacity.
- Press states: **darken by two steps** + `transform: translateY(1px)`.

### Transparency &amp; blur
- **Avoid blur effects.** A single sanctioned use: 60–80% white scrim over the navy hero when overlaying text on photography.
- Transparency on white over navy uses `rgba(255,255,255,0.78)` for muted text.

### Card patterns
- White card, 1px `--border`, radius `--radius-lg` (14px), padding 24–32px, no shadow at rest.
- On hover: shadow `--shadow-md`, border darkens to `--border-strong`. No transform.
- "Featured" cards on navy backgrounds use a subtle `rgba(255,255,255,0.08)` fill with `rgba(255,255,255,0.12)` border.

## 4. Iconography

The supplied materials contain **no proprietary icon set** — the brand book and product guidelines rely entirely on the wordmark, the CM monogram, and product-color dots. There are no SVG icons, icon fonts, or sprite sheets in the source files.

**Approach used in this design system:**
- **Icons via [Lucide](https://lucide.dev) (CDN)** — clean 1.5px stroke, rounded caps, neutral. Closest match to the brand's clinical-but-humanist register. **This is a substitution and is flagged below.**
- Icon color: `currentColor`, defaulting to `--cm-blue-500` on light, white on navy.
- Icon size: 16 / 20 / 24 / 32px. Stroke `1.75` for 16/20, `2` for 24/32.
- **Emoji: never.**
- **Unicode glyphs**: the typographic em-dash `—`, registered mark `®`, and the bullet `•` are the only sanctioned non-icon glyphs.
